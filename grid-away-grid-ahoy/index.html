Dinoco

Dinoco, fueling your future!

Dinoco is an oil company/gas station that has been seen in the Toy Story and Cars franchises. In Toy Story, the logo is a Brontosaurus. In Cars, the logo is a Tyrannosaurus Rex. At Disney California Adventure, Cars Land also features Dinoco gas pumps at Flo's V8 Cafe. The pumps' logo features a Stegosaurus.
